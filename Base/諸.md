# 諸
various, many, several, together

### Kun-Yomi

### On-Yomi
ショ

### Mnemonics
日 -> sun, day
言 -> say, speech
土 -> alien (crossed over earth)

To describe other planets of the solar system ***in words*** one could call them the ***various and many*** non-earths near sun
